Product Design



As a product designer, the design process is an integral part of creating successful
products. It begins with understanding the needs and wants of the customer, then
researching and brainstorming ideas to create a design that meets their needs. Once the
design has been created, it’s important to test and refine the product to ensure it meets the
customer’s expectations. This includes creating prototypes, conducting user testing,
creating wireframes and making necessary changes to the design.
